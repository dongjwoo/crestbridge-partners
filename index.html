Our Approach

Three questions decide a U.S. launch.

Who pays. Who specifies. Who implants.
We answer them in that order — and we don't move past one until the next is in clear focus.

U.S. commercialization is won at the cath lab and in the payer review, not at the trade show. CrestBridge brings the operational discipline of a global medtech commercial team to founders who haven't built one yet.
